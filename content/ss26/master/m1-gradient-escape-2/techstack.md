## Asset creation
**[Krita](https://krita.org/)** — Used for creating game assets, including new HUD elements and other visual designs.

**[FL Studio](https://www.image-line.com/)** — Used for composing and producing the game's background music and sound effects.

## Development
**[Unity Engine](https://unity.com/)** — The core engine used for building, testing, and running the game.

**[JetBrains Rider](https://www.jetbrains.com/rider/)** — Used as an IDE for C# development within Unity.

**[Github](https://github.com/)** —  Used for version control, issue tracking, and pull request reviews.

## Organization & Communication
**[Google Sheets](https://www.google.com/sheets/about/)** — Used for task management, tracking sprint progress and assigning issues across the team.

**[Google Forms](https://www.google.com/forms/)** — Used to collect responses from public testers during the larger testing rounds.

**[Discord](https://discord.com/)** — Used for day-to-day team coordination, quick questions, and hotfix discussions.

**[Slack](https://slack.com/)** — Used for communication with the professor and gathering her feedback.

**[Zoom](https://zoom.us/)** — Used for weekly meetings.
